Chop-wrap transform your text nodes

A small browser module to apply transformations to DOM text nodes

By default, it can recursively chop words or letters and wrap them in a custom tag

  • Vanilla JS
  • IE>8

Can be useful to animate text, transform DOM text nodes into something else or whatever.

Enough talk, a little demo (with the help of velocity.js for animations)
